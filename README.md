# Audit-Risk-Analytics-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analysing audit observations, risk ratings, and audit progress using real-world audit data.


📊 Audit & Risk Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

🔍 Project Overview

This project presents an Audit & Risk Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyse audit observations, risk ratings, and audit progress across multiple business areas.
The dashboard enables risk-based insights, helping stakeholders monitor audit effectiveness, control weaknesses, and compliance status through interactive visualisations.

The analysis is based on a Food Chain Audit dataset, simulating real-world internal audit and operational risk scenarios.

🎯 Objectives

Analyse audit observations and risk areas

Monitor audit status (Completed, In Process, Planned)

Identify high-risk observations requiring management attention

Track audit progress by branch, region, and time

Support risk-based audit decision-making

📊 Dashboard Highlights
🧾 Audit Observations Analysis

Breakdown of audit observations such as:

Completeness of financial records

Inventory and food safety controls

Regulatory compliance issues

Security and pest control gaps

Percentage-based comparison to identify most significant risk areas

🚦 Audit Status Tracking

Clear view of audit progress:

✅ Complete

🔄 In Process

🗓 Planned

Helps management track pending and overdue audits

⚠️ Risk Rating Analysis

Analysis of risk ratings vs number of instances

Identification of high-frequency and high-impact risks

Monthly trend analysis of:

Risk significance

Audit findings volume

🗓 Time & Regional Analysis

Audit timelines by branch and region (East, West, North, South)

Comparison of earliest end dates and average risk percentages

Supports resource planning and audit scheduling

📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)

Total Audit Observations

High / Medium / Low Risk Counts

Audit Completion Status (%)

Risk Significance by Month

Branch-wise Audit Performance
